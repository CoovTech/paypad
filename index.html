Manage your Stripe account from your iPad

The first iPad app for the Stripe API

Intuitive mobile payments.

Accept payments anywhere!

Developers love Stripe for its super simple API. Forget merchant accounts and gateways and throwing away your hard earned money on stupid fees. Stripe just works, and it works insanely well. PayPad for Stripe takes that same Stripe API and makes it equally as awesome by letting you manage your Stripe acount on a beautiful iPad interface. Now you can manage your Stripe account and take payments wherever you can take an iPad (caution: iPads are not waterproof... believe me, I checked).

Card charges.

With PayPad for Stripe you can view all of your recent charges, starting with the 20 most recent transactions. Do you have more than 20 transactions? Of course you do, because you smell like success. Load up as many historic charges as you want. View the details of every charge. Want to make someone's day? Refund a customer's payment out of the goodness of your heart.

View your customers.

Access your customer data while on the go! You can view your customer list, create new customer charges, and even refund existing charges.

See your bank transfers

Easily reconcile recent charges to your bank account with the Transfers view. See the transactions that are pending transfer and also transactions that have successfully been deposited into your account.

Access event logs

Events represent a real-time log of your account activity. You can view your entire event history for the last 30 days.

Safe and secure

PayPad for Stripe uses OAuth to authenticate with Stripe. We can't see your account credentials so you can rest easy that your Stripe account stays safe and secure. Are you using PayPad right now? I don't know because OAuth is sneaky good!
