PayPad for Stripe

iPad app for the Stripe API

Developers love Stripe for its simple API. No merchant account. No Gateway. Just 0-60 in no time flat. PayPad takes that same Stripe API and lets you manage your Stripe account on a beautifull iPad interface. Now you can manage your Stripe account and take payments while on the go!

Charges

With PayPad, you can view all of your recent charges. We begin with the 20 most recent and go from there. You can load as many historic charges as you want. You can view the details of every charge. Need to refund a customer's payment? We've got you covered!

You can also create new charges on the fly, or add charges to your existing customer accounts. Perfect for those who sell products, not just from their website, but also in a store or out in the field.

Customers

Access your customer data while on the go! You can view your customer list, create new customer charges, and even refund existing charges.

Transfers

Easily reconcile recent charges to your bank account with the Transfers view.

See the transactions that are pending transfer and also transactions that have successfully been deposited into your account.

Events

Events represent a real-time log of your account activity. You can view your entire event history for the last 30 days.

Safe and Secure

PayPad uses OAuth to authenticate with Stripe. We never see your account credentials so you can rest easy that your Stripe account stays safe and secure.
