PayPad for Stripe

iPad app for the Stripe API

Developers love Stripe for its super simple API. You don't need no stinking merchant account. You don't need no stinking gateway. It just works, and it works insanely well. PayPad takes that same Stripe API and makes it equally as awesome by letting you manage your Stripe acount on a beautiful iPad interface. Now you can manage your Stripe account and take payments wherever you can take an iPad (caution: iPads are not waterproof... believe me, I checked).

Charges

With PayPad you can view all of your recent charges, starting with the 20 most recent transactions. Do you have more than 20 transactions? Of course you do, because you smell like success. Load up as many historic charges as you want. View the details of every charge. Want to make someone's day? Refund a customer's payment out of the goodness of your heart.

You can also create new charges on the fly, or add charges to your existing customer accounts because they keep calling you at 2am asking if Google is working. PayPad even works for those who sell products out of the trunk of their car; this isn't just for website sales, but is field usable as well!

Customers

Access your customer data while on the go! You can view your customer list, create new customer charges, and even refund existing charges.

Transfers

Easily reconcile recent charges to your bank account with the Transfers view.

See the transactions that are pending transfer and also transactions that have successfully been deposited into your account.

Events

Events represent a real-time log of your account activity. You can view your entire event history for the last 30 days.

Safe and Secure

PayPad uses OAuth to authenticate with Stripe. We can't see your account credentials so you can rest easy that your Stripe account stays safe and secure. Are you using PayPad right now? I don't know because OAuth is sneaky good!
